Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish with its origins among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, or fish), or in some cases, without any meat (vegetarian), and sometimes, in certain varieties, eggs and/or vegetables such as potatoes.
