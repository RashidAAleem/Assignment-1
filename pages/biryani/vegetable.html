Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines assorted vegetables with fragrant rice, spices, and herbs. It's a popular dish in South Asian cuisine, loved for its rich taste and vibrant colors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, potatoes, bell peppers), diced - about 2 cups
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (coriander), chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and soak in water for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a large pan or pot, heat ghee/oil. Add cumin seeds and sliced onions. Saute until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saute for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil starts to separate.
  5. Add assorted vegetables. Cook on medium-high heat until vegetables are slightly tender.
  6. Add plain yogurt and mix well.
  7. Add soaked rice and gently mix with the vegetables and spices.
  8. Add enough water (about 3 cups) to cover the rice. Bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat to low, cover with a lid, and cook for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  10. Garnish with chopped cilantro and mint leaves.
  11. Serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) and salad.
